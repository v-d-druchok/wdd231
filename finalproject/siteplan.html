Project Site Plan

Efficient Training Edge Overview

Site Name

I chose the name 'Efficient Training Edge' because it is the first thing that came to my mind when I tried to come up with a name for a fictional company for this project.

Site Purpose

The site provides useful tips, info, and advice on nutrition and training, as well as a form through which customers can reach out with specific inquiries and such.

Scenarios

  • What is the best approach to cutting with the goal of losing as little muscle as possible?
  • What sort of training split should I do as a beginner?
