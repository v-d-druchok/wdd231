How many grams of protein should I eat per day?

Generally the optimal amount of protein would be 0.7-1 gram per lb of body weight. With a slight exception being that if you are on a cut, you should aim a little higher to prevent muscle loss, with 0.9-1.5 grams per pound of body weight being recommended.

How hard should I go on a bulk?

Our personal recommendation for anyone out there who's looking to gain as much muscle as possible while limiting fat gain to a minimum would be to go on a lean bulk of about 200 to 400 calories above maintenance.

How do I find my maintenance calories?

The best way to find your caloric maintenance would be to first look up some sort of calculator that does that online. Give it your weight, height, and age, and eat whatever the calories it displays for a week. If after a week you gain a little weight, adjust your calories by a few hundred and eat like that for a week. If you lose a bit of weight after that week, add a couple hundred calories to your diet. Rinse and repeat until your weight stabilizes.
