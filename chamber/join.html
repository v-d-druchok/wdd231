NP (Non Profit) Level

Free level of membership. Includes access to all basic events and features.

Bronze Level

Entry-level membership. Includes access to all basic events and features plus the ability to be featured on the directory page of the commerce website as well as access to exclusive member events.

Silver Level

Mid-level membership. Includes access to all basic events and features plus advertising in the form of being featured on the directory and home pages of our website, access to exclusive member events, and exclusive member training sessions./p>

Gold Level

High-level membership. Includes access to all basic events and features plus advertising in the form of being featured on the directory and home pages of our website and exclusive sponsor banners at our in-person events, access to exclusive member events, exclusive member training sessions, and early access to the latest information regarding all things commerce.
